Gang Wars

Thug Pugs Gang Wars in an on-chain play-to-earn game that pits the Swanky Rebels against the Dead Bone Locos. Use strategy and combat skills to conquer and control territory, and earn bags of $TBONE.

Whitepaper

Meet the Swanky Rebels

In the beginning, the Swanky Rebels appeared on the block with swagger and shiny ear bling. They came in peace, but it didn't last. There weren't enough $TBONES for their flamboyant living. Rebellion broke out among the Swankies, and the gang's code was corrupted.

Fear the dead bone locos

With the morals of sewer rats, they renounced the Swankies by cutting their own ear, ditching their ear bling. The ritual alone reveals the badass nature of a Loco. Locos live to steal as many $TBONES as they possibly can, even if they have to break some bones along the way.

Which side are you on?

Are you a Swanky or
are you a Loco?

Swankies and Locos became the Yin and Yang of Thug Pugs. The balance of forces is always at risk, as the gangs fight for territory in a race to hodl the greatest amount of $TBONES to fuel their lifestyles.
