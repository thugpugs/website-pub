About

Pugs seemed doomed to live as fancy props — mere portable delights for their human masters. But a few rogue ones didn't want to play the nice-dog game anymore.
They are the Thug Pugs, and although they all seem adorable to the eye, their territorial nature quickly emerged to make things ugly, and gangs soon emerged.
